Roots and Wanderlust:

Although I was born in the vibrant city of Pune, my roots trace back to the historical town of Munger in Bihar. However, owing to the dynamic nature of my father's profession, our family embraced the beauty of diversity by calling various cities home. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the scenic landscapes of Srinagar, the military life took us to places like Hashimara - a small town on outskirts of West Bengal, historical city of Agra , until we finally settled in the joyful city of Vadodara.

Early Influences:

Growing up amidst these diverse cultures and landscapes has significantly influenced my worldview. It taught me adaptability, the value of embracing change, and the richness that comes with experiencing different lifestyles.

Schooling Adventures:

My educational journey has been just as eclectic as my upbringing. From the vibrant school days in different corners of the country, each institution brought unique experiences and friendships. Navigating the Tech Landscape:
